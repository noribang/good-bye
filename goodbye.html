Goodbye

A program to bid our friends farewll all over the world.

  • English: Goodbye, friends!
  • Spanish: Adios, amigos!
  • Japanese: Sayoonara!
  • French: Au Revoir!
  • Kinyarwanda: Murabeho Mwirirwe! (afternoon); Muramuke Turongera! (evening)
  • German: Auf Wiedersehen!
  • Pig Latin: Oodgay yebay!
